– LAPACK auxiliary routine (version 3.1) – Univ. of Tennessee, Univ. of California Berkeley and NAG Ltd.. November 2006

Purpose

DLADIV performs complex division in real arithmetic

a + i*b p + i*q = ------— c + i*d

The algorithm is due to Robert L. Smith and can be found in D. Knuth, The art of Computer Programming, Vol.2, p.195
