Detailed Description

– LAPACK routine (version 3.1) – Univ. of Tennessee, Univ. of California Berkeley and NAG Ltd.. November 2006

Purpose

DLARZ applies a real elementary reflector H to a real M-by-N matrix C, from either the left or the right. H is represented in the form

H = I - tau * v * v'

where tau is a real scalar and v is a real vector.

If tau = 0, then H is taken to be the unit matrix. /summary>

Purpose

DLARZ applies a real elementary reflector H to a real M-by-N matrix C, from either the left or the right. H is represented in the form

H = I - tau * v * v'

where tau is a real scalar and v is a real vector.

If tau = 0, then H is taken to be the unit matrix. /summary>
