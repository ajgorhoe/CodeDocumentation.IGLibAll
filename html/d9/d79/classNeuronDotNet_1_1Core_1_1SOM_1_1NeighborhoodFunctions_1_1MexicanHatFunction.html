Detailed Description

Mexican Hat Neighborhood Function is the normalized second derivative of a Gaussian function. It is a continuous function with neighborhood value decreasing from unity at the winner to a negative value at a certain point (forming an inhibitory influence) and then gradually increasing to zero.
