Detailed Description

– LAPACK auxiliary routine (version 3.1) – Univ. of Tennessee, Univ. of California Berkeley and NAG Ltd.. November 2006

Purpose

DLAZQ3 checks for deflation, computes a shift (TAU) and calls dqds. In case of failure it changes shifts, and tries again until output is positive.

/summary>
