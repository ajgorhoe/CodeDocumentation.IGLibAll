Trains the network for the given training sample (Online training mode). Note that this method trains the sample only once irrespective of the values of currentIteration and trainingEpochs. Those arguments are just used to adjust training parameters which are dependent on training progress.

Parameters
trainingSampleTraining sample to use
currentIterationCurrent training epoch
trainingEpochsNumber of training epochs
Exceptions
ArgumentNullExceptionIf trainingSample is null
ArgumentExceptionIf trainingEpochs is not positive, or if currentIteration is negative or if currentIteration is less than trainingEpochs
